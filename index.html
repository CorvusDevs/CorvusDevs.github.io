Our principles

How we build software

🔒

Privacy by default

No analytics, no tracking, no accounts. Your data stays on your device. We don't want it and we don't collect it.

🍎

Native to macOS

Built with Swift and SwiftUI, optimized for Apple Silicon. Our apps feel like they belong on your Mac because they do.

💰

Pay once, own it

No subscriptions, no recurring fees. Buy once and it's yours forever. We think that's how software should work.

⚡

Performance first

Every app is engineered for minimal resource usage. We obsess over CPU cycles and memory so your Mac stays fast.

🎯

Focused tools

Each app does one thing exceptionally well. No feature creep, no unnecessary complexity. Just the right tool for the job.

🤝

Human support

Real responses from real people. Have a question or found a bug? Email us directly and we'll get back to you.
