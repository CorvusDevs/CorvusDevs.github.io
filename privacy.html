Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 30, 2026 · Last Updated: March 30, 2026

CorvusDevs ("we", "us", "our") develops and publishes software applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data across our products and services.

Our Commitment

We believe in minimal data collection. Our applications are designed to work locally on your device whenever possible, without requiring accounts, telemetry, or cloud services.

Data Collection

Our applications do not collect, store, or transmit personal data unless explicitly stated in a product-specific privacy notice. In general:

  • No analytics or telemetry — We do not track how you use our apps
  • No personal information — We do not collect names, emails, IP addresses, or device identifiers through our apps
  • No third-party tracking — We do not embed advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or tracking pixels in our apps
  • Local storage only — App preferences and settings are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server

Payments

Purchases of our products are processed by third-party payment providers (such as Paddle). These providers handle billing, taxes, and invoicing on our behalf. We do not directly receive or store your payment details. Please refer to your payment provider's privacy policy:

  • Paddle: paddle.com/legal/privacy

License Validation

Some of our paid products may communicate with a payment provider's servers to validate license keys. This communication is limited to license verification and does not transmit personal data beyond what is necessary for that purpose.

Website

Our website (hosted via GitHub Pages) does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts.

Product-Specific Notices

Individual products may have supplementary privacy information documented on their respective pages. Where a product-specific notice exists, it takes precedence over this general policy for that product.

Children's Privacy

Our products are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last Updated" date.
